About this website

This website provides a forum for FAQs and a place where "Whispers" in the corridor are formally put down in order to ensure that correct information is disseminated and is available to everyone.

Who answers these questions?

A designated group of seniors of the school (Grade 11 and above) will be answering these questions. The website is moderated by a teacher.

How can i submit my questions?

You can submit your questions under the "Query" tab on this webpage, and they will be answered as soon as possible.
